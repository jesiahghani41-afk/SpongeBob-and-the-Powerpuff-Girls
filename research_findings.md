# Research Findings: Oggy and the Cockroaches Parody

## Oggy and the Cockroaches Core Elements
- **Protagonist (Oggy):** A blue cat who just wants to relax, watch TV, and eat. He is often the victim of the cockroaches' pranks but can be quite capable when pushed.
- **Antagonists (The Cockroaches):**
    - **Joey:** The leader, small, purple-headed, and the brains behind the schemes.
    - **Marky:** The tall, green-headed one, often distracted by love or vanity.
    - **Dee Dee:** The blue-headed one, obsessed with food.
- **Sidekick (Jack):** Oggy's cousin, a green cat who is more aggressive, tech-savvy, and often tries to "help" Oggy with disastrous results.
- **Neighbor (Bob):** A huge, tough bulldog who lives next door and often gets caught in the crossfire of the chase, usually resulting in him beating up Oggy or Jack.
- **Tone:** Slapstick, non-verbal (mime-based), fast-paced, and surreal.

## Character Mapping (Preliminary)
- **Oggy Role:** SpongeBob SquarePants (The optimistic but easily bothered homeowner).
- **Cockroach Roles:** The Powerpuff Girls (Blossom, Bubbles, Buttercup).
    - **Blossom as Joey:** The leader and strategist.
    - **Bubbles as Dee Dee:** The cute one with a specific obsession (maybe sugar/candy instead of just food).
    - **Buttercup as Marky:** The tough one who might have a vanity or competitive streak.
- **Jack Role:** Patrick Star (The "helpful" best friend whose interventions often make things worse).
- **Bob Role:** Squidward Tentacles (The grumpy neighbor who just wants peace but gets pummeled).

## Episode Formula
1. **The Setup:** SpongeBob is enjoying a peaceful activity (flipping burgers, jellyfishing, or just relaxing at home).
2. **The Intrusion:** The Powerpuff Girls arrive and start causing chaos/pranking SpongeBob for fun or out of boredom.
3. **The Escalation:** SpongeBob tries to stop them, leading to a series of slapstick chases and increasingly complex traps.
4. **The Crossfire:** The chase spills over into Squidward's yard, leading to him getting hurt and retaliating.
5. **The Climax:** A massive, surreal set-piece involving high-speed movement or explosive results.
6. **The Resolution:** SpongeBob ends up in a comical state of defeat, while the Girls fly off giggling.
